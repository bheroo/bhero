What is the Difference Between Bhero and Maiar Launchpad?

There are not many differences between the two. But, of course, Maiar Launchpad, as a giant, will remain the leader. We both have similar end goals: To bring only legitimate crypto-related companies to the Elrond Community for a brighter future.

We do not see, and would never see, Maiar Launchpad as a competition, nor do we see other Launchpads as competition, such as Polkastarter, UFO Gaming, DAO Maker, BSCpad, and so on.

Our ultimate goals are to bring more attention and adoption to the crypto sphere.

Frequently asked questions

What is BHero?

BHero is a cryptocurrency and NFT launchpad helping the most disruptive and practical projects start their journey into web3. Whether an established web2 business or a completely new company looking to join blockchain in a legally compliant manner, BHero assists in every aspect.

How can I participate?

Participating in future launchpad projects on BHero will need you to hold and stake BHAT, then connect your wallet here. Look out for more details about upcoming projects on our Telegram group .

How many BHAT Tokens do I need at stake?

To be eligible for ticket allocation, you need to have a minimum of 1000 $BHAT staked.

Where do I need to stake BHAT?

The only place that you can currently stake BHAT is .

Do I also need EGLD?

Staking EGLD is optional and will provide small increases in your BHero level.

BHAT is the primary governance token of the BHero Launchpad, but out of respect for the Elrond community, we have also decided to provide ~10% of the power to EGLD stakers.

What is the level of BHero?

The new ticket allocation system, designed by the BHero team, considers the time that holders have staked their tokens and the number of tokens staked. Click here to read the technical article explaining the process.

If I stake more, will my level increase?

Yes, the more BHAT you have staked, the higher your daily EXP increase. The EXP formula is the following:

Gross Daily Experience Rate = (BHAT Stake Daily Rate + MGStaking EGLD Stake Daily Rate + Any SP EGLD Stake Daily Rate) + NFT Bonus.

Will you also host NFT sales rounds?

Yes.

Does Bhero have its own token?

No. BHero uses $BHAT, the token of BH Network.

What is BH Network, and why does BHero use the same token?

BH Network is a cryptocurrency hub designed for freelancers who want to remove the pain points that come with current FIAT platforms. BHero is a secondary company of BH Network, bringing more development onto the Elrond blockchain.

BHAT will be the governance token of both projects, providing perks to freelancers on the marketplace, entrepreneurs, and investors on the Launchpad.

What countries are allowed to participate?

The approved and restricted countries for each Launchpad project are not in the control of BHero. However, we respect and enforce the legal requirements of each project and its legal entity jurisdiction.

If your country is on the restricted list for one project, it might not be for the next project.

How many projects will you incubate per year?

There is no solid rule for how many projects the BHero team will incubate during any given time. We focus on quality, not quantity. Therefore, the time space between projects will differ; but there will not be more than one project per month on our Launchpad.
